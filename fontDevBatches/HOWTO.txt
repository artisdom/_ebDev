To batch convert fonts, re: http://fontforge.org/scripting-tutorial.html :

Open a command shell, and type, for example:

	fontforge -script convert.pe --format ".otf" *.ttf

This will convert every .ttf font file in the command's immediate path to .otf font files (so, the paramater in quote marks is the desired target format).

This works with virtually *any* font format, including the .sfd font projects native to FontForge!
